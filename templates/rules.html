Game rules

Objective

The player aims to lay out all the cards in the round. If they are the last one to fail to do so, they lose the round. The game continues until one of the players loses a total of three rounds.

Setup

The game can be played by 2 to 4 players and it uses a deck of 24 cards (9, 10, W, D, K, A). The following card seniority applies: A – K – D – W – 10 – 9.

Each player receives an equal number of cards. Each player only sees their cards.

The game begins with the player with the 9 of Hearts, placing it face up in the middle. It remains in the middle until the end of the round.

How to play

  • The player places a card of equal or greater value than the last one placed in the middle.
  • If they have 4 cards with the same value greater than the one in the middle, they can lay them down in one move (the exception is 9 - then the player lays down three).
  • If a player does not have or does not want to play a card of equal or greater value, they must take the three most recently placed cards from the middle. If there are fewer than three cards in the middle, the player takes all but the 9 of Hearts.
